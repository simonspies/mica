**Mica/Stdlib**

- `Combinators.lean` — Embeddings and the pure-intrinsic builders (`Pure.Unary`/`Pure.Binary`) that emit an intrinsic and its soundness instance, plus the shared helper lemmas their soundness proofs use.
- `IntStd.lean` — Integer-arithmetic intrinsics (`Int.min`, `Int.max`) and their soundness instances.
- `StringStd.lean` — Byte-string intrinsics (`String.length`, `cat`, `equal`, `starts_with`, `ends_with`) and soundness instances.
